# MINDSTER
MINDSTER - YOUR VERY OWN MIND BUDDY

Mindster is a mental health wellness app, designed with an aim to provide an interactive, user-friendly and comfortable environment with a safe space to express, feel and do whatever you like!

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/66664643/173538628-31167e42-257f-4402-8f40-c1f2caae54e0.png" alt="splash screen" width="200" height="400">

### TECH STACK:
Java, Firebase, Alan AI

### 4. INFO SECTION 

A goto section to gather knowledge on all the different types of mental health issues one might be facing. Valid and verified information on sensitive topics like PTSD, anxiety, ways to curb them, the famous 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique, everything provided at one place!

### 5. RELAX-STATION

Feeling perplexed? Not able to take hold of your thoughts? Come running to our relax-station section! With a bunch of mediation guides, calming sounds, deep sleep music, your mind will be transported to a land of only peace and no chaos.

### 6. DEAR DIARY

Coz sometimes when we don't feel like explaining our thoughts to anyone, we write! A personal, private journal section for the user to pen down his/her daily thoughts.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/66664643/173543935-622dec0f-d0ad-4a2b-a8f8-24caf290d152.png" alt="info screen" width="200" height="400">


### 7. MOOD TRACKER

Tracking your mood helps in better understanding of your emotional states and ultimately improves your mental well-being. Not only can these tools be useful for determining if mental health interventions are helping with your mood, but they can also help you see how your mood might influence your physical health!
